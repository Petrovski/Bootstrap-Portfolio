About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Alex, welcome to my portfolio home page. I was born and raised by 1st generation immigrant parents in south east Michigan. I first got into web development in early 2018, where I used a course on uDemy by Colt Steele to gain a basic knowledge in a lot of areas pertaining to development. I really enjoyed it and thought of it not as studying or work, but more like leisure time. Because of this feeling I got while coding, I had really wanted to turn this into something more. I wanted to make it into something I could do for a living, freelancing or working for a company.

Having some experience with a previous online course in web devleopment, I knew that taking this bootcamp would solidify my chances for getting this going for real. It's important to find something you love doing, and making a living in life. That is the real end goal in my opinion, to be happy and healthy for a long time.
